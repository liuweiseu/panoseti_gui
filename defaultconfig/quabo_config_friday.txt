*CHANNEL MASK BITS
*EACH bit of each 32b CHANMASK word, when 1, disables one trigger channel
*CHANMASK_0[0] is MAROC0 (U1) channel 0, 
*CHANMASK_7[31] is MAROC3 (U4) channel 63, etc
*CHANMASK_8[7:0] are the OR-trigger bits (2 from each MAROC)
*CHANMASK_8[8] is the external trigger input (connected to other QUABOS)
